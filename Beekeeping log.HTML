Welcome, welcome to the log of bees!

Come with me to enter the realm of the bee lifestyle and dive into the honey-tastic adventures and journeys bees travel. The are three roles that a honey bee is genetically assigned. Those are the drone, queen and worker bees.These roles have an essential benefit to the animal kingdom. Providing food to humans all over the globe.

What are worker, queen and drone bees?

A worker bee is a standerised bee with gathers pollen, produces honey and nurse the larvae to become one of these three roles

The queen is the bee which takes care of the reproduction by having drones impregnate them, making 5-6 eggs every minute.

The drone is used for fertilising the eggs in the queen, so then new larvae can be taken care of and can become the new generation of Bees.
